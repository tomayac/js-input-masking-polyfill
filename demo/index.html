There are commonly agreed-on rules how credit card numbers should be formatted. Try it with one of the test credit card numbers below. Note that this does not verify credit card numbers for validity, it purely masks them according to the rules for each credit card vendor.

(Needless to say: no information you enter is shared with the server.)

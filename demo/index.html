There are commonly agreed-on rules how things like credit card numbers or International Bank Account Numbers (IBAN) should be formatted. Try the demo with one of the numbers below. Note that this does not verify the numbers for validity, it purely masks them according to the rules for each type.

(Needless to say: no information you enter is shared with the server.)

Intl.InputMask("credit-card number").format()
